At the 2026 Conference for Undergraduate Women and Gender Minorities, I had the amazing opportunity to ``Game Master" for an Adventures with Emmy playtest: an NSF-funded tabletop roleplaying game for STEM education. Tabletop roleplaying games are a great way to practice improvisation skills. Combined with the STEM themes, Adventures with Emmy is perfect for practicing science communication or even just getting comfortable casually conversing in the language of STEM. If you'd like to try Adventures with Emmy for yourself, you can check out the game and guides [here.](https://adventureswithemmy.domains.unf.edu/)
